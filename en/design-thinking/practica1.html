Management and Problem Statement

After making a great variety of proposals, we come together in an agreement, which will help society and be profitable.

Proposal

The human sector of the third age represents a significant percentage for Peru. Many older people are alone and bored at home, some of these people would like to meet new people online, but because of their age they do not know where to start since they do not have the basic knowledge. Our project has as one of the main pillars to help this type of people to know people to talk and why not say that they look for partners through social networks. As a team we have enough skills to be able to offer this type of personalized help to the elderly who are willing to learn and be able to beat the technology.

The older adult in the face of new technologies.

Social and Economic Impact

There are a variety of computer or office computer science academies, however, there are not so many that are specialized in teaching seniors, since most work with children or with university students. In conclusion, our project would have a wide range of clients with whom we could work.
