Empathize

It is the first step we must take to know what a consumer / client needs. So it is necessary to conduct a market study, conduct surveys, etc., to be able to clearly determine how we are going to solve our problem.

Market Study in our Region

In order to determine how we should structure our project, we must look for entities that are similar, so we went to look for the closest ones in order to have an idea of ​​what these entities offer to the market.
